San Jose Sharks Playoffs 2019

It is hard to watch a game in the 2019 Playoffs without hearing the fans chanting “Ref you suck” or booing an infraction against the away team.

The San Jose Sharks have been shrouded in controversy earlier in the playoffs in Game 7 of Round 1 against the Vegas Knights. The now-infamous major call on the cross-check that took out Sharks Captain Joe Pavelski until Game 7 of the next round. The call became a hotly debated topic after the Sharks went on to score 4 goals within the 5 minute major, coming back to win the series in overtime.
